To truely go broke buying plastic

If you want to print in 4 to 16 colors just throw money at an Automatic Material System (AMS) or four. The AMS can connect to P1 and X1 printers. The AMS allows the printer to switch between colors automatically through out the print.

The A1 and A1 Mini have an AMS Lite for up to four colors for those who prefer the bed slinger printers. Like the X1 and P1 series, the A1 has a 256mm3 build volume. The A1 Mini has a still impressive 180mm3 build volume.
